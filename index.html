About Us

Discover the story behind TSUEE FHOP: Coffee and Hugs.

Founded in 2023 by a group of friends with a shared passion for coffee and a genuine belief in the power of human connection, TSUEE FHOP is not just a café; it's a place where the aroma of freshly brewed coffee mingles with the warmth of heartfelt hugs. The concept originated from the idea that a simple cup of coffee has the potential to foster meaningful connections and spread joy.
